About Me

Hi Im Rick Romero. I was born and raised in Southern California. I've lived my whole life in California and I am proud to be a true Californian. I am the youngest of four siblings. During the day I am a tanker driver, which means I get to drive a truck and trailer that weighs about 80,000 pounds and is loaded with highly explosive fuel. I have been a truck driver for the last 12 years. I've seen a lot and I've done a lot. The most common question I get asked is, "Aren't you scared of blowing up?" My reply is always the same "I'm concerned, never scared. I'm cautious, never afraid".

On my down time I like to hang out and train at the local boxing gym or spend some time with my two small dogs that I consider my children. I also like to compose, write and produce music. I'm a fan of the rockabilly scene and from time to time I like to head out to a good show and catch up with old friends. Other hobbies I enjoy doing are restoring my 1967 Pontiac Catalina. I have a love and hate relationship with this vehicle. I love it when everything is working and performing right, but I hate it when something breaks down and I have to fork out the cash to get it fixed. I also enjoy traveling. I try to travel as much as possible. There is something about going to another country and experiencing the culture and its history. It always makes me feel like im in a different world and it's something I cant get enough of.
